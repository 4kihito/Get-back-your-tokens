Get back your tokens

But first, connect your wallet!
IMPORTANT! The order of the transactions must be followed. The 'unstake' function call, unstakes the other tokens, after this you can withdraw the DaoMaker tokens.

First Transaction

The first transaction that we have to send is unstake to get the remaining NON DaoMaker tokens from the vault.

If Metamask is showing absurd amount of gas estimate, most likely you already have sent a unstake transaction, than you can skip it and try sending the withdraw transaction directly.

Second Transaction

We just need to call the withdraw function and you will be getting back your DaoMaker tokens.
